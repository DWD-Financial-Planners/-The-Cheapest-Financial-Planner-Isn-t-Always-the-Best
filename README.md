## 📊 How Financial Planners Typically Charge in South Africa

Financial planning fees generally fall into three categories:

### 1️⃣ Once-Off Consultation
- R1,000 – R2,500
- Ideal for basic financial guidance

### 2️⃣ Comprehensive Financial Plan
- R5,000 – R15,000
- Includes full retirement, risk, and investment strategy

### 3️⃣ Ongoing Advisory Fees
- 0.5% – 1% of assets under management
- Includes portfolio reviews and continuous strategy adjustments

Value comes from structure, expertise, and long-term support — not simply price.
